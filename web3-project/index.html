Mission Statements

At ELCLCs,our mission is to enhance English Language and Computer skills in seven Afghan Universities by providing instruction in English Language through Computer Assisted Language Learning, office automation and Internet skills so that Afghan Universities’ faculties and students could keep up the global standards.

Introduction

English Language and Computer Learning Centers (ELCLCs) is a U.S. Embassy funded project, which is being implemented by Educational Support Organization (ESO), a non-profitable Afghan local NGO. ELCLCs operate in seven Afghan universities namely Kabul University, Kabul Polytechnic University, Balkh University, Herat University, Kandahar University, Nangarhar University and Sheikh Zayed University (Khost). The ELCLCs project is the successor to the Afghan eLearning English Support Project (AeLESP), funded by the U.S Embassy until Jan 31st, 2011, which itself was the successor of the Afghan e-Quality Alliances (AeQA), funded by USAID until Dec 31st, 2010. Herat University ELCLC was established in 2008 by AeQA project and so far has managed to train over 4000 Students, Faculty and University staff in English and ICDL.
